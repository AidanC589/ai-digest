## Try This
Now that Claude Code v2.1.215 no longer auto-runs `/code-review`, add it as a deliberate step in your end-of-task ritual: after Claude finishes implementing a feature, explicitly prompt `/code-review` followed by `/verify` before committing. This turns what was passive behaviour into an intentional gate — giving you a consistent, auditable checkpoint rather than relying on Claude to decide when review is warranted. You can also chain them in a single turn: finish the implementation prompt, then immediately follow with `/code-review` and ask Claude to surface any security concerns specifically before you `/verify`.
