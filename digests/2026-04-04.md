## Try This
In Claude Code, run `/cost` after your next non-trivial session — the new v2.1.92 update now breaks down costs per model and shows cache-hit rates separately. Use this to identify which parts of your workflow are cache-missing (burning uncached tokens) and restructure your system prompt or conversation flow to keep stable context at the top where it will be cached on subsequent turns. If you're seeing high uncached token counts on returning sessions, the new footer warning will now tell you exactly how many tokens will go uncached on your next turn, giving you a concrete signal to `/clear` and restart rather than continuing an expensive stale session.
