- [AI is potentially a Dunning-Kruger effect amplifier](https://twitter.com/i/status/2066825204207091926) — The observation that AI can confidently regurgitate confused claims from prior users ("midwit glazing") is a concrete prompt-hygiene concern: AI-generated code reviews and explanations on specialist topics may sound authoritative while being subtly wrong, reinforcing the need for human domain-check passes.
