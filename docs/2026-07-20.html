General AI Developments

AI advice made people less accurate but more confident – study
Researchers found AI availability collapsed willingness to say "I don't know" from 44% to 3%, dropped accuracy to one-third of baseline, while confidence nearly tripled. Directly relevant to AI code review: if you're using AI to audit AI-generated code, you may be systematically more wrong and less aware of it.
HuggingFace security incident report: "the attacker was bound by no usage policy, while our own forensic work was blocked by the guardrails"
HuggingFace was hit by an autonomous AI agent attack; their own forensic AI analysis was blocked by commercial API safety guardrails when processing real exploit payloads, forcing them to run open-weight models locally. Key security finding: commercial frontier models are unsuitable for incident response on real attack data.
