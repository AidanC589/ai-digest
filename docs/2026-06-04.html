RAG label framing shifts LLM adoption of wrong answers by 56–84 percentage points
a controlled study across GPT-5.5, DeepSeek V4 Pro, and others shows that wrapping context with Instruction: or Reference: dramatically increases the model's adoption of injected misinformation, while Example: suppresses it. Direct implication for anyone building RAG pipelines or prompt templates that feed AI coding assistants with retrieved docs.

across 10 datasets and 4 retrieval methods, retrieval barely moved the needle; the model's ability to use evidence was the bottleneck. Applies broadly: if your AI code review pipeline retrieves docs or examples, the model you choose will dominate retrieval quality.
